# Codify Starter Kit

## 4 files. That's it.

These 4 reference files are the context layer between your business and AI. Fill them in once — every AI tool you use gets better immediately.

---

## The Files

| File | What It Does | Time to Fill |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| `soul.md` | WHY you exist — your origin, beliefs, principles | 15 min |
| `offer.md` | WHAT you sell — products, mechanism, transformation | 20 min |
| `audience.md` | WHO buys — real people, real language, real pain | 20 min |
| `voice.md` | HOW you sound — tone, phrases, personality | 15 min |

**Total: ~70 minutes to build your permanent AI context.**

---

## How to Use Them

### Step 1: Fill in the templates
Open each `.md` file and replace the `[bracketed prompts]` with your actual business knowledge. Be specific — the more precise you are, the better AI output gets.

### Step 2: Paste into any AI session
Start your next ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini session by pasting the contents of all 4 files. Then ask it to generate whatever you need — ads, emails, scripts, content.

### Step 3: Notice the difference
The output will sound like you. Not generic. Not assistant-speak. You. Because AI finally has context instead of guessing.

### Step 4: Keep refining
Every time you learn something new about your audience, make a decision about your offer, or refine your voice — update the files. The context compounds over time.

---

## Tips

- **Don't overthink it.** A rough first draft is 10x better than no context at all. You can refine later.
- **Use your customers' words.** Especially in audience.md — their language becomes your ad copy.
- **Soul.md is the hardest.** That's normal. Start with offer.md or audience.md if you get stuck.
- **These work with ANY AI tool.** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Mistral, Llama — markdown is universal.
