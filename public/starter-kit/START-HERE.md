# Codify Starter Kit

## Two ways to build your context

### Option A: AI Interview (recommended — 30 min)

1. Open `codify-starter-prompt.md`
2. Copy everything below the line
3. Paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI chat
4. Answer the questions — AI walks you through all 4 files
5. Save the completed files

The AI will interview you about your business and build all 4 reference files from your answers. No blank page. No guessing what to write. Just answer questions about what you already know.

### Option B: Fill in the templates (70 min)

Open each template file and replace the `[bracketed prompts]` with your business knowledge:

| File | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| `soul.md` | WHY you exist — origin, beliefs, principles |
| `offer.md` | WHAT you sell — products, mechanism, transformation |
| `audience.md` | WHO buys — real people, real language, real pain |
| `voice.md` | HOW you sound — tone, phrases, personality |

---

## What to do with your completed files

Paste all 4 into the start of any AI session. Then ask it to generate whatever you need — ads, emails, scripts, content. The output will sound like you because AI finally has context instead of guessing.

**Keep refining.** Every time you learn something new, update the files. The context compounds over time.
